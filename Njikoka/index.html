About Njikoka LGA

Njikoka Local Government Area is located in the central part of Anambra State, Nigeria, with its administrative headquarters in Abagana. The LGA comprises the communities of Abagana, Abba, Enugwu‑Agidi, Enugwu‑Ukwu, Nawfia and Nimo. The people are predominantly Igbo and are known for their strong town‑union structures, active religious life and rich cultural festivals.

The official Njikoka LGA website documents the evolution of the area from earlier local councils and customary courts into the current Local Government structure, which works in partnership with Anambra State Government and federal agencies to deliver services.
