About Orumba South

Discover the rich heritage and dynamic economy of one of Anambra's most promising local government areas

Location

Situated in the southeastern part of Anambra State, Nigeria, with Umunze as the administrative headquarters. Strategically positioned along major trade routes connecting the state's commercial centers.

Population & Demographics

Home to over 200,000 vibrant residents across 195 square kilometers. The area is characterized by a youthful, entrepreneurial population with a rich Igbo cultural heritage and strong community bonds.

Historical Context

Created in 1996, Orumba South LGA has evolved from a predominantly agrarian society into a dynamic commercial hub. The area's history is deeply rooted in traditional commerce, farming excellence, and educational advancement, making it a cornerstone of Anambra's economic development.
