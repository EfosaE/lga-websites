Brief Overview

Oyi is a Local Government Area in the Anambra North Senatorial Zone. Created in 1991, it has evolved from a purely agrarian society into a vibrant commercial hub. It serves as a critical connecting point between the agricultural interior and the commercial cities of Onitsha and Awka.
