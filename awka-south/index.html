About Awka South

Awka South LGA is one of the most important administrative and commercial regions in Anambra State. It is home to the **Anambra State Government House**, state ministries, educational institutions, corporate offices, and vibrant markets. Awka South consists of major towns including **Awka**, **Amawbia**, **Nibo**, **Nise**, **Mbaukwu**, **Ezinato**, **Ezinifite**, **Isiagu**, and **Okpuno**.
