Economic Sector Analysis

Awka South has a unique "hybrid economy." Unlike agrarian LGAs, Awka South is driven by the Civil Service, Education, and Commerce sectors.

  • Commerce (Eke Awka) 40%
  • Public Sector / Govt 35%
  • Education & Services 15%
  • Agriculture 10%

Strategic Infrastructure

Government House

Location: along Enugu-Onitsha Expressway. The administrative heart of Anambra State.

Nnamdi Azikiwe University

A federal university that drives the local real estate and service economy in Ifite-Awka.

Eke Awka Market

The largest retail market in the city, serving as the central trading hub.

Blacksmith Heritage

Awka is historically famous for metal works, a tradition still celebrated today.

Flyover Bridges

Modern flyovers at Aroma, Kwata, and Amawbia junctions ease capital traffic.

Hospitality Sector

Highest concentration of hotels and convention centers in the state.
