About Onitsha South

Onitsha South Local Government Area is one of the most commercially vibrant local government areas in Anambra State, Nigeria. Located in the southeastern part of the state, it serves as a major economic nerve center for the entire region.

Established as part of the administrative restructuring of Anambra State, Onitsha South has grown to become a bustling commercial hub, hosting some of the largest markets in West Africa, including the famous Ochanja Market which attracts thousands of buyers and sellers of variety of commodities daily.

The LGA is strategically positioned along the Niger River, providing excellent access to waterways and connecting various economic zones across Nigeria. Its proximity to the Onitsha Main Market makes it a critical component of the region's trade ecosystem.

Economic Overview

Onitsha South is predominantly commercial, serving as a major trading hub in West Africa

Trading & Commerce

Wholesale and retail trading of textiles, electronics, automotive parts, building materials, and general merchandise

Light Manufacturing

Small-scale manufacturing of plastics, furniture, metal works, and processed foods

Import/Export

Major import and distribution center for goods from Asia, Europe, and other African countries

Artisan Services

Tailoring, welding, carpentry, automobile repairs, and other skilled trades

Hospitality

Hotels, restaurants, event centers catering to the large number of traders and visitors

Financial Services

Banking, microfinance, money transfer services, and currency exchange

Major Commercial Markets

Ochanja Market

One of the largest markets in West Africa, specializing in textiles, clothing, footwear, and fashion accessories. Attracts thousands of buyers and sellers of variety of commodities daily.

Market Days: Daily

Electrical/Electronics Market

Major distribution center for electrical appliances, electronics, and telecommunications equipment

Market Days: Daily

Ose Okwodu Market

Traditional market for foodstuffs, provisions, and household items

Market Days: Every 4 days (Eke day)

Pharmaceutical Market

Specialized market for pharmaceutical products and medical supplies

Market Days: Daily
