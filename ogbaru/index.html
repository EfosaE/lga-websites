About Ogbaru

Ogbaru Local Government Area is strategically located in the southern part of Anambra State, Nigeria. It serves as a major commercial hub along the Niger River, connecting various parts of the state and neighboring regions.

Created in 1996, Ogbaru has grown to become one of the most economically vibrant local governments in Anambra State. The area is known for its bustling markets, thriving river commerce, and agricultural productivity. The proximity to the Niger River has made Ogbaru a strategic location for trade and transportation.

With an estimated population of over 228,000 people and covering approximately 450 square kilometers, Ogbaru is home to diverse communities engaged in trading, farming, fishing, and various commercial activities.
