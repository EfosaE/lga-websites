Agricultural Data

Agriculture is central to Orumba North's economy. Key crops include:

  • Yam (widely cultivated and a staple)
  • Cassava and cocoyam
  • Rice (wet and upland types)
  • Palm produce (oil palm, kernels)
  • Vegetables and maize

Farmers also engage in poultry, goat and pig rearing, plus fishing in available water bodies.

Economic Activity & Commercial Index

Orumba North's economy combines agriculture with trade, education‑related services (due to Federal Polytechnic Oko) and small‑scale industries. Markets operate on traditional Igbo market days (Eke, Oye, Afor, Nkwo), serving local traders and farmers.

Major commercial activities include farm produce trade, building materials, transport services and retail shops catering to students and residents.
