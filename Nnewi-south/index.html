Overview

Location

Nnewi South is located in the southern region of Anambra State, Nigeria. It is strategically positioned near the industrial hub of Nnewi, sharing boundaries with Nnewi North, Ekwusigo, Ihiala, and Orumba South Local Government Areas.

Historical Context

Nnewi South Local Government Area was carved out of the former Nnewi Local Government in 1996 as part of Nigeria's local government reforms. The area has a rich Igbo heritage and has been home to industrious communities known for trading, agriculture, and craftsmanship for centuries.

The people of Nnewi South are predominantly Igbo-speaking and are known for their entrepreneurial spirit, which has contributed significantly to the commercial and industrial development of the region. The LGA serves as an important agricultural and trading zone, supplying food and raw materials to the wider Nnewi industrial area.

With its strategic location and industrious population, Nnewi South continues to play a vital role in the economic landscape of Anambra State.
