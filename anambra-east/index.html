Overview

Anambra East is situated in the northern part of the state, along the banks of the majestic Omambala River. It is an agricultural powerhouse, known for massive production of yam, cassava, and fish.

With its headquarters in Otuocha, the LGA is a blend of ancient tradition and bustling commerce. It holds a significant place in history as the ancestral home of the Igbo people (Aguleri).

  • HQ: Otuocha
  • Zone: Anambra North
  • Hub: Agriculture
  • Heritage: Fr. Tansi

Culture & Destinations

Otuocha Market

The economic heartbeat of the Omambala. Famous for its massive yam tubers and fresh river fish sold every four market days.

Ovala Aguleri

A royal festival attracting tourists globally. It showcases the rich heritage of the Aguleri dynasty and Igbo kingship.

Fr. Tansi Shrine

A pilgrimage site in Aguleri dedicated to Blessed Cyprian Michael Iwene Tansi, attracting thousands of faithfuls annually.
