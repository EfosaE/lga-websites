Economic Drivers

Idemili South operates a dual economy. The northern axis (Oba) is heavily commercial, driven by the International Market and proximity to Onitsha. The southern axis (Nnobi, Awka-Etiti) is famous for transport commerce and medium-scale enterprises.

  • Commerce (Oba/Nnobi): The dominant sector, featuring auto-parts and transport hubs.
  • Real Estate: Rapidly growing residential estates serving the Onitsha metropolis.
  • Agriculture (Ojoto/Alor): Palm oil production and subsistence farming.
